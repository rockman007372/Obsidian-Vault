## Definition

>[!definition]
> A combination is a selection of r objects out of n, without regard to the order. 

## Formula
${{n}\choose{r}} = \frac{n!}{r!(n - r)!}$

${{n}\choose{r}} = {{n}\choose{n - r}}$

${{n}\choose{r}} = {\frac{^{n}P_{r}}{r!}}$
