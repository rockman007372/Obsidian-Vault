### Habit Tracker

```dataview
TABLE WITHOUT ID
  file.link as Date,
  choice(sleep-hours > 6, "✅", "❌") as Sleep,
  choice(breakfast > 0, "✅", "❌") as Breakfast,
  choice(study-hours >= 2, "✅", "❌") as Study,
  choice(workout-hours > 0, "✅", "❌") as Workout
  
FROM "Daily" AND [[2024-W36]]
SORT file.day ASC
```
