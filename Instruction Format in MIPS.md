## Notes
There are 3 types of MIP instructions:
- R-instructions → Register
- I-instructions → Immediate
- J-instructions → Jump

### R-instructions
- Add
- Sub
- Logic with register: and, or
- Shift operations: sll, srl

### I-instruction
- addi
- lw/sw
- logic with immediate: andi/ori/xori
- branch: beq, bne
